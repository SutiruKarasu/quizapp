About Me

Tatiana Lumos is a Prague-based photographer creating cinematic portraits and visual stories inspired by emotion, contrast, and inner transformation.

Tatiana is my real name.
Lumos is a pseudonym that, for me, became a symbol of light, intuition, and the quiet magic hidden within everyday life.

I am inspired by contrasts in all their forms: softness and strength, closeness and distance, light and darkness, beauty and tension. I am drawn to images that hold emotion, ambiguity, and space for interpretation — photographs that invite you not only to look, but to feel.

My work exists at the intersection of fashion, portraiture, and emotional storytelling. I photograph women’s portraits, couples, family stories, and artistic projects — anything where not only the image matters, but also the atmosphere, emotion, and inner state of the person.

For me, photography is not about creating a perfect image, but about the opportunity to see yourself differently — more daring, sensual, tender, free, or even unfamiliar to yourself.

I am especially inspired by people who feel that there is something more within them — another side of their personality, an emotion, or an energy waiting to be expressed. Photography becomes a space where this hidden part can emerge and become visible.

I am based in Prague and open to travel projects.
